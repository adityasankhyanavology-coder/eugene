Origin Story

Every story begins somewhere — this is mine.

What youll find here...

Youll find fiction about the search for meaning in a world that can feel meaningless. Its about the tension between who we want to be and who we are. Its about the struggle to find our place in the world and the beauty of the journey, not just the destination.

Somewhere along the way, I didn t want to craft some perfect brand.

I wanted to write stories that were true to my experience. I wanted to write about the messy, complicated, and beautiful parts of life. I wanted to write about the things that people are afraid to talk about.

Elaborate roadmaps have been my M.O....

...for as long as I can remember. The van build, the business plan, the 5-year plan. Ive always been a planner. But the one thing I couldnt plan for was the story that was unfolding inside me. The one that was begging to be told.

Using a pen name is interesting...

Its a way to create a new identity, a new persona. Is a way to separate the art from the artist. For me, it was a way to give myself permission to write the stories I wanted to write without fear of judgment. It was a way to be vulnerable without being exposed.

FROM THERAPY TO FICTION

As a therapist, I help people rewrite their stories. As a writer, I do the same. Fiction is a way to explore the human condition, to understand ourselves and others better. Its a way to find meaning in the chaos and to connect with something larger than ourselves.

The fiction on this site is an extension of that work. Its a way to continue the conversation about what it means to be human. Its a way to explore the big questions and to find hope in the darkness. Its a way to connect with you, the reader, and to let you know that youre not alone.
