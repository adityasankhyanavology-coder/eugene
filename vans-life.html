Mile Markers

Mile Markers are little stories from the road — quiet moments between gas stations and sunsets. You meet strangers at dusty diner counters, pass through towns that barely exist, and discover highways that change you without saying a word. Every mile leaves a memory, and every turn carries a story waiting to be told.

Rest Areas

Rest Areas are where we pause, breathe, and reset. These are the quiet corners beside loud freeways — a thermos of coffee in cold morning air, a map unfolded on the dashboard, a moment to think before the next mile. Sometimes stopping is the only way to move forward.

Stories built on long highways, late-night diners, and quiet corners of America. Tales of wanderers, dreamers, and the roads we take to find ourselves.
